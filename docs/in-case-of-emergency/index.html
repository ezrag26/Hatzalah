I called Hatzalah because I didn’t feel well. I didn’t think I needed to go to the hospital, it was Shabbos, I just wanted to get a medical opinion. One of the volunteers, who lives on my block, picked up on an abnormal finding in my belly. He convinced me to go get checked out. It turns out I had an abdominal aortic aneurysm, a rare and potentially fatal condition. He saved my life that night. - Rachel
